TRUST

Built with privacy, restraint, and church trust in mind.

My Kingdom Pal is designed for spiritual formation and care connection, which means trust cannot be an afterthought.

PRIVACY

Private reflection stays private by default.

Journal entries and mood notes are designed to remain on the member’s device.

CARE

Church follow-up happens when members choose to share.

Prayer and support requests create a path for care connection only when a member intentionally reaches out.

PRODUCT POSTURE

No engagement hacks.

The product is intentionally non-gamified and does not try to turn discipleship into visible performance metrics.

CHURCH ROLE

The church remains central.

My Kingdom Pal is built to support the church’s teaching and care rhythm, not replace leadership or ministry relationships.

IMPORTANT

This is not a full church management platform.

The current product positioning is focused on sermon-linked weekly rhythm, private reflection, and care connection. It should not imply admin dashboards, analytics, workflow automation, or real-time access to private member notes.
