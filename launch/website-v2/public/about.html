ABOUT

Built to support the church’s voice, not compete with it.

My Kingdom Pal exists to help churches create more continuity between Sunday teaching, weekday formation, and moments when care is needed.

VISION

Help believers live the Word every day, beyond Sunday.

We want churches to have a calmer, more faithful bridge between what is preached and how members walk it out during the week.

ORIGIN

The problem is familiar to almost every church.

Sunday can be strong, clear, and spiritually meaningful. The harder question is what happens on Monday, Tuesday, and beyond. My Kingdom Pal is being built around that gap.

CONVICTIONS

Formation over friction

We are not building a product around streaks, pressure, or visible spiritual performance.

CONVICTIONS

Private by default

Reflection and mood notes are designed to remain on the member’s device unless they choose to share a care-related request.

CONVICTIONS

Church-led, not platform-led

The local church should remain the center of teaching and spiritual leadership.

CONVICTIONS

Care matters

When members ask for prayer or support, the path toward follow-up should be clearer and more intentional.

LOOKING AHEAD

We’re building toward a church-facing launch, not a generic consumer app release.

The goal is not to replace discipleship strategy or church leadership. The goal is to support the church’s existing rhythm with a more coherent weekly experience.
